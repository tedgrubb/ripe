 781388 -- CHICKEN  CURRY

3 med. potatoes
4 tbsp curry powder (or more if desired)
8 ounce sour cream
2 large cooking onions
2" piece fresh ginger
3 cloves garlic
Salt to taste
5 tbsp cooking oil
1 cup water

 Cut the cooking onions, ginger and garlic into smaller pieces.  Put them into a food processor and mince finely.  Skin and cut potatoes into quarters.
